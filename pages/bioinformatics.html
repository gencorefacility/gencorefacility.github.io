What We Do

The Gencore bioinformatics team provides high-throughput sequencing data analysis with speed, automation, and reliability across multiple platforms. We offer comprehensive support spanning data processing through archival.

Team

  • David Gresham — Faculty Director of Bioinformatics
  • Mohammed Khalfan — Senior Bioinformatics Specialist
  • Eric Borenstein — Biology Department HPC Specialist

Supported Platforms

Illumina (MiSeq, NextSeq, NovaSeq), Oxford Nanopore (MinION, GridION), and Element Biosciences (AVITI).

Core Services

  • Automated analysis pipelines and quality control reporting
  • Data delivery via HPC cluster and permanent archival
  • Support for advanced and custom sequencing library designs
  • Metadata traceability through our LIMS system
  • Full integration with NYU HPC cluster through Open OnDemand web interface
  • Nextflow/nf-core pipeline support
