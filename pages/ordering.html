KAPA qPCR Reagent Form

To order KAPA qPCR Reagents through GenCore, please use the KAPA qPCR Reagent Request Form.

Researchers can purchase a full set of reagents, which includes:

  • KAPA master mix (enough for 20 libraries)
  • One (1) set of standards (enough for one qPCR run)
  • One (1) plate
  • One (1) plate film

Researchers can also purchase just a set of standards, which includes:

  • One (1) set of standards (enough for one qPCR run)
  • One (1) plate
  • One (1) plate film

Consumables Request Form

Select consumables are available for purchase through GenCore. Please use the GenCore Consumables Request Form for all requests.

Some of the consumables available for purchase include:

  • Agilent TapeStation High Sensitivity DNA D1000 ScreenTape (single tape)
  • Agilent TapeStation High Sensitivity DNA D1000 Sample Buffer
  • Agilent TapeStation High Sensitivity DNA D1000 Ladder
  • Agilent TapeStation High Sensitivity RNA ScreenTape (single tape)
  • Agilent TapeStation High Sensitivity RNA Sample Buffer
  • Agilent TapeStation High Sensitivity RNA Ladder
  • Countess Cell Counting Chamber Slide (single slide)
  • Invitrogen Trypan Blue Stain (0.4%, 1 mL tube)
