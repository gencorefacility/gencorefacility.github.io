Platforms

GenCore currently operates three different Illumina sequencing platforms, one Oxford Nanopore Technologies sequencing platform, and one Element Biosciences sequencing platform across our two locations.

  • ONT GridION
  • Element BioSciences Aviti
  • Illumina NovaSeq 6000
  • Illumina NextSeq 500
  • Illumina MiSeq
