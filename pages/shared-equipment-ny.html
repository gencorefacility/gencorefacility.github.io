Researchers MUST be trained on the following equipment before operating. Sign up for a training session on BookitLab or email GenCore to request a training.

GenCore instruments are grouped into the following categories:

  • Analysis & QC Instruments
  • Flow Cytometry
  • High-Throughput Instruments
  • Sonicators
  • General Use Equipment

Analysis & QC Instruments

GenCore is equipped with several auxiliary instruments for assessing sample viability, quality, and concentration.

Agilent BioAnalyzer 2100

The Agilent Bioanalyzer 2100 is a micro-fluidics based platform that can analyze the quantity, quality, and characteristics of small RNA, DNA, protein and cell samples.

GenCore has two Agilent Bioanalyzer 2100 systems:

  • 8th Floor, CGSB
  • 8th Floor Brown Building

Quick Reference Guide

Agilent TapeStation 4200

The Agilent TapeStation 4200 is a high throughput automated electrophoresis tool for analyzing nucleic acid sample quality and fragment length. It offers scalable throughput, is fast and easy to use, and provides researchers with great reproducibility.

GenCore has two Agilent TapeStation 4200 systems on 8th Floor, CGSB.

Quick Reference Guide

Amersham Typhoon RGB

The Amersham Typhoon RGB is a robust and high throughput biomolecular imager that can be used for fluorescence scanning, phosphorimaging, digitization and chemiluminescence experiments.

Location: Waverly Building – Room 766

Quick Reference Guide

Bio-Rad CFX96 RT-PCR Thermal Cycler

The CFX96 qPCR instrument is a powerful and flexible system that combines advanced optical technology with precise temperature control.

Location: Waverly Building – Room 766

Quick Reference Guide

Bio-Rad ChemiDoc MP

The Bio-Rad ChemiDoc MP is an imager that can visualize and analyze proteins and nucleic acids across a wide range of gels and blots, including western blots. The ChemiDoc MP can analyze stain-free gels and blots, capture high sensitivity chemiluminescence, or perform multiplex fluorescence analyses using a wide variety of gel stains (such as ethidium bromide, SYBR, and Coomassie).

GenCore has two Bio-Rad ChemiDoc MPs:

  • 3rd Floor, CGSB
  • Waverly Building – Room 766

Quick Reference Guide

Countess II FL

The Life Technologies Countess II FL is an automated cell counter with florescence capability. This allows for cell counting, fluorescent protein expression monitoring, and measuring of cell viability.

**EVOS Light Cubes Available: GFP, RFP, & DAPI

Location: 8th Floor, CGSB

Quick Reference Guide

NanoDrop Spectrophotometer

The NanoDrop Spectrophotometer allows the quantification of microvolumes. The Department has a number of these units, including one in the Shared Equipment Core, for general use.

Location: 8th Floor, CGSB

Quick Reference Guide

QuantStudio 6 Flex Real-Time PCR

The QuantStudio 6 Flex Real-Time PCR system contains a 384 well block and is capable of performing qPCR, Genotyping, Melt Curve, and Comparative Ct experiments, plus other applications. The system supports SYBR Green and TaqMan reagents, among others.

Location: 8th Floor, CGSB

Quick Reference Guide

Roche LightCycler 480

The Roche LightCycler 480 system consists of a thermal cycler used for rapid real-time quantitative PCR and SNP detection, a computer to control the thermal cycler, and software for the design of probes for hybridization probe analysis and primers for SYBR green analysis.

Location: 8th Floor, CGSB

Quick Reference Guide

Flow Cytometry

There are two high throughput flow cytometers available to sort and analyze various cell types using fluorescence. Technician run cell sorting is also available for researchers.

BD FACSDiscover S8 Cell Sorter

The FACSDiscover S8 is a high throughput spectral flow cytometer and cell sorter capable of visualizing and analyzing cells in real-time. The system is equipped with five lasers (blue, red, violet, yellow-green, and ultraviolet), 78 fluorescent detectors, and 6 imaging detectors. The S8 is capable of 6-way sorting into tubes, as well as plate sorting.

**Only technician-run appointments are available.

Location: 8th Floor, CGSB

Quick Reference Guide

Cytek Aurora

The Cytek Aurora is a spectral flow cytometer equipped with 4 lasers (V/B/YG/R) and 48 detectors, and is capable of detecting the emission full spectra of samples.

*PLEASE NOTE training for this instrument requires at least 48 hours notice before the training appointment. An experimental form must be filled out and controls and/or test samples should be brought to the training. Contact GenCore for additional information.

Location: 8th Floor, CGSB

NanoCellect Wolf G2 Sorter

The NanoCellect Wolf G2 Cell Sorter is a benchtop cell sorter capable of bulk cell sorting or single cell sorting into 96-well or 384-well plates. This instrument is equipped with 488 nm and 561 nm lasers and can process up to nine colors. This is a self-serve instrument once training is complete.

*PLEASE NOTE training for this instrument requires at least 48 hours notice before the training appointment. Contact GenCore for additional information.

Location: 8th Floor, CGSB

High-Throughput Instruments

GenCore has a number of instruments used to perform various repetitive tasks in high throughput, including NGS library preparation, normalizations, and cell-based fluorescence assays.

10X Genomics Chromium Controller

The 10X Genomics Single Cell Chromium Controller is a benchtop instrument for single cell capture and gene expression profiling. The 10X Chromium can capture hundreds to millions of cells with a simple workflow, and users can go from cell sample to sequencing library in less than one workday.

Location: 8th Floor, CGSB

Quick Reference Guide

10X Genomics Chromium iX Controller

The 10X Genomics Chromium iX Controller is the updated version of the Chromium. This instrument delivers the same single cell partitioning capabilities of the Chromium, but with greater efficiency and reproducibility. The Chromium iX has the ability to run many of the GEM-X and Next GEM assays.

Location: 8th Floor, CGSB

Agilent Bravo Robotics System

The Agilent Bravo Robotics system is a high throughput liquid dispensing robot. It is equipped with a 96 pipette head and a flexible deck that can accommodate Agilent NGS Option B protocols for library preparation, plate stamps, PCR setups, and much more.

**Please contact GenCore if there is a protocol/project you wish to automate.

Location: 8th Floor, CGSB

Quick Reference Guide (along with a list of currently validated protocols!)

Tecan Spark Microplate Reader

The Tecan Spark Microplate Reader is a multimode plate reader that can be used with various plate types ranging from 24-well plates to 384 well plates, allowing scalability for experiments. It is equipped with a high speed monochromator aiding in absorbance, fluorescence, luminescence detection, and more.

Location: 8th Floor, CGSB

Quick Reference Guide

Sage Blue Pippin

The Sage Blue Pippin can target a wide range of molecular sizes (90bp to 50kb) for use in preparing NGS libraries. 5 samples can be run in parallel with no risk of cross-contamination.

Location: 8th Floor, CGSB

Quick Reference Guide

Sonicators

There are three different types of sonicators available to researchers for various types of experiments including DNA shearing for NGS, chromatin shearing, tissue homogenization, and more.

Covaris S220

The Covaris S-Series Focused Ultra Sonicator is a versatile, high power system that performs at high frequencies for a variety of applications.

Location: 8th Floor, CGSB

Quick Reference Guide

Diagenode Bioruptor Pico

The Diagenode Bioruptor Pico sonicators can uniformly process multiple samples and can be used to shear DNA and chromatin as well as homogenize tissue.

GenCore has two Diagenode Bioruptor Pico Instruments:

  • 8th Floor, CGSB
  • 8th Floor Brown Building
